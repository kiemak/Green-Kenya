About the Organization
GREEN KENYA MOVEMENT is a foundation that was formed with the purpose of
makng our county a green country by planting as many trres as possible and also by taking care of our water catchment areas.
The founder who is a young Kenyan have a goal of making Kenya green by the year 2030.
The foundation has organized many tree planting events in various forests that we have and also
establishing new forests. By now two years down the line since the movement was started we have managed to plant 9 million trees
countyrwide. Yes we are doing great though more need to be done. Next year we have a target of planting 6 million trees before the year ends.
This can be achieved if we join hands and accept to make Kenya green as from your place of stay.
We do encourage schools, private companies, foundations and individuals at large to come and join hands with us so as to achieve this together.

The founder of this organization is Kibe Kiema,
who is very much determined to help our country achieve the goal of 20% forest by 2030.
God bless the organization.
